Benefits

  1. Encouraging Cognitive Development
    2.

    When learning to code, such as making a simple game, children's cognitive abilities will develop. These cognitive aspects include the ability to solve problems, logical thinking skills, critical thinking skills, and systematically can be honed.

  2. Sharpen Computational Thinking
    3.

    By learning to code, children can improve their ability to understand the concept of algorithms. So, they can learn to solve problems will develop rapidly. Then, they can break it down into concrete, workable steps.

  3. Increase Creativity
    4.

    Learning to code can increase children's creativity. Moreover, various processes and variations in finding solutions in the coding process encourage children to think outside the box. They are encouraged to use imagination in developing a game or application. For example, when learning to display images, or even making an application development innovation. This is what can trigger their creativity.

  4. Practicing Persistence, Patience, and Courage
    5.

    We can also l earn about persistence, patience, and the courage to try something new in the app creation process. In every application development, it is natural for everyone to make mistakes. Now, with abilities such as systematic, logical, and creative thinking, children can also be more persistent in trying to create something new and solve existing problems. These abilities are a package of soft skills abilities that children can get when learning coding.
